clviz

ClarityViz's pip-installable package, clarityviz or clviz (nickname pronounced “seel-viz”), is a Python-based library that allows neuroscientists to visualize CLARITY brains in different ways. clviz converts raw .nii and .img files into CSV's and graphML files, and then converts these files into appropiate charts that the user can select.

Using clviz, we are processing brain scans on an ongoing basis, and the resulting graphs are made available to the community through our server here.
